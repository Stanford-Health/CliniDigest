Date: 2025-04-03

The Polar Verity Sense, a versatile and accurate optical heart rate sensor, serves a pivotal role in psychiatry trials by providing real-time, non-invasive physiological data crucial for assessing the impact of various interventions on mental health. In trials examining the effects of supplements on stress markers [1], the influence of music on nursing student stress [2], the impact of acetaminophen on fear responses [3], and the benefits of high-intensity interval training on depressive symptoms in older adults [4], the Polar Verity Sense can offer invaluable insights into the physiological underpinnings of psychiatric conditions and the efficacy of therapeutic interventions, thereby enhancing our understanding and management of mental health.
